Monty Hall Problem 02/17

In attempt to generate a solution (or rather reproduce the already proven solution) for the famous Monty Hall Problem, I coded a simple simulation of the problem with C++ that would run through a great number of iterations to test the success of the differing strategies. Ultimately, I was able to reproduce the historically accepted results for myself, and I had a lot of fun in the process.

Basketball Simulation Game 11/16

To practice for my first term C++ course when I was in the Systems Design Engineering course, I developed a command line basketball simulation game making use of class based design. The game places you in a late game situation of a close game and has you take control of your team. By instructing your players, whose abilities vary, to pass or shoot, you may attempt to overcome the opponent, reclaiming the lead by climbing back from a deficit, or holding a lead to claim the victory when the time expires. This game was inspired by this video from Sal Khan of Khan Academy: https://goo.gl/UHsVnL
