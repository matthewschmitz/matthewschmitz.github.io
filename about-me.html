About

My name is Matthew Schmitz and I am a first year Honours Biomedical Sciences student at the University of Waterloo. I am a passionate learner whose interests span from calculus and chemistry to astrophysics and literature to hip-hop and the NBA. Most of my time is divided between experimentation on new projects, reading both for enjoyment and to learn new things, and playing basketball.

I intend to use this site as a space to share the projects I have on the go and the ones that I have completed, as well as share some of my thoughts and feelings on topics I find interesting.

I coded this simple site after I read Jon Duckett's HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery books.
